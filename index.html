Background
This project was completed as part of an internship practice and is based on an existing finance-related project. The purpose of the project was to analyze a real-world banking application and recreate it for educational and practical learning purposes. Bank of America was chosen as a reference due to its strong presence in digital banking and its focus on mobile-based financial services.
Problem
Many young users do not see significant differences between traditional banks and are increasingly interested in innovative financial tools. Current banking applications often provide only basic features, such as payments and expense tracking, and lack personalized financial management tools. This creates a challenge for banks to remain relevant to younger generations.

Project Goals
The goal of this internship project was to design and recreate a personal finance management feature for young professionals. The project focused on helping users better understand and manage their finances while allowing the intern to gain practical experience in product analysis, feature design, and financial application development.
